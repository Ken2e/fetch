frequently asked questions

what are payment methods?

electronic transfers and crypto can be use as payment method.

what time can i check-in and check-out?

standard check-in time is at 11 AM and check-out at 11 PM. kindly note that check-in may be shifted during high occupancy periods. while for sailing the captain will communicate the sailing time.

how can i talk to the duty manager?

please dial service key "1" from your in-room phone for assistance.

do you have wifi?

stay connected during your stay with 80MB bandwidth internet service provided throughout our guestrooms and public areas. please login with your username and password provided at check-in.

do you offer airport shuttle service?

airport transfers to or from the airport start at just €2,500 per trip.

do you accept corporate events and accomodations?

for corporate, group, and event enquiries please email sales@thegrandpalace.com
